Co-founded Khayaal - Microsoft Imagine Cup regional Winners 2021

Khayaal is a comprehensive solution to verify and digitize prescribed medicines and tackle the risk of counterfeit. KHAYAAL verifies medicines using computer vision for people who own a smartphone. For people without a smartphone or who cannot read or write we have offline KHAYAAL services, Doctors can also prescribe the medicine to the patient using KHAYAAL. KHAYAL finds the nearest pharmacy with the required medicine. It also delivers your required medicine within 30-Minutes of you ordering it.
